Interesting facts about pigeons

  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

Fun Loving facts about Teddy bear 😆 💗

  • The world's largest stitched teddy bear is proudly American! The 55-foot-4 creation was made in 2008 and is named C.T.Dreams, which stands for Connect the Dreams. You can go visit it in Wichita, Kansas!
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.

Funny facts about camels

  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Hilarious things about Pandas!

  • Giant pandas grow to between 1.2m and 1.5m, and weigh between 75kg and 135kg.
  • Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm – that’s about the size of a pencil!
  • These beautiful bears are endangered, and it’s estimated that only around 1,000 remain in the wild. That’s why we need to do all we can to protect them!
